A few helpful tips when booking an Airbnb:

  • Choose places that have plenty of reviews and read those reviews carefully! Remember to review cancellation policies just in case.
  • Use the filters in order to find the best fit for your preferences. Guest count can affect the costs.
  • Availability subject to change. The suggested Airbnb accommodations on our wish lists were still available to book as of Saturday, August 5.
  • If you decide to stay outside of the Highland Park area or the surrounding neighborhoods (Pasadena, Eagle Rock, Mount Washington, Glassell Park) please consider driving time and TRAFFIC.
  • If all of this is confusing, please feel free to book at the Westin Pasadena or any of the surrounding hotels.
